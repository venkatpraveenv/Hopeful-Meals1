ABOUT FOOD RESCUE

Turn surplus food into shared meals, not waste.

Food Rescue connects supermarkets, restaurants, farms, and local charities so that perfectly good food reaches families instead of landfills. Donors list surplus food, charities reserve what they need, and together they coordinate pick‑ups in minutes.

  • Donors create time‑sensitive listings of surplus food.
  • Charities discover and reserve donations that match their needs.
  • Real‑time chat keeps donors and charities aligned on every booking.
